Integrated schools in Hartford in 2017-18
Several schools in Hartford have been targeted to improve diversity, but the large majority have not been targeted and remain racially and ethnically isolated. Schools marked green are considered integrated, with a black and Hispanic population below 74 percent. Schools marked red have a black and Hispanic population of 75 percent or higher. Schools marked yellow have black and Hispanic populations between 74 and 75 percent and are in danger of becoming segregated.

Source: State Department of Education
